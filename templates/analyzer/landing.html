How It Works

Research-Backed Sleep Science

Every metric mapped to published sleep medicine research on PSQI, polysomnography, and wearable biosignals.

❤️

Heart Rate & HRV

Resting heart rate is inversely correlated with sleep quality. Elevated nocturnal HR indicates autonomic dysregulation and poor recovery.

🫁

SpO₂ Monitoring

Blood oxygen drops below 95% signal potential sleep-disordered breathing. Our model detects apnea risk patterns from SpO₂ trends.

🌡️

Skin Temperature

Core body temperature drops 1-2°C during quality sleep. Distal skin warming (hands/feet) is a biomarker of healthy sleep onset.

⚡

EDA (Sweat Activity)

Electrodermal activity reflects sympathetic nervous system arousal. High EDA before/during sleep disrupts NREM and REM architecture.

🏃

Active Zone Minutes

Moderate daily exercise increases slow-wave sleep by 15%. Our model factors in timing and intensity for accurate sleep scoring.

🤖

Gradient Boosting ML

GBM outperforms linear models on tabular biometric data. Trained on 80K realistic synthetic samples with stratified cross-validation.
